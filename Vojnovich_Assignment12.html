About Me

Sarah is a student at Wake Technical Community College studying Advertising and Graphic Design. She is also a Notary for Hospice throughout the Triangle (Raleigh, Durham, Chapel-Hill) and a freelance photographer, graphic designer, social media coordinator, and web designer. Sarah has a Bachelor of Science in Communication Studies (emphasis in Public Relations) and a minor in Art from Wingate University. In addition to her degrees, she has multiple certifications in Paralegal Studies, Notary Public, Clinical Chiropractic Assistant, and various Adobe programs.

Sarah currently resides in Cary, NC, and loves to help make people's dreams come true.
